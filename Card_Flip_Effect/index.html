Durga

Known as: Goddess of Power, Strength and Protection. Other Names: Adi Shakti, Mahishasura Mardini, Bhagavati, Bhavani, Jagadamba. Weapon: Chakra, Shankha, Trishula, Gada , Bow and Arrow, Khanda, Padma. Details:Durga (Sanskrit: दुर्गा) is a major Hindu goddess, worshipped as a principal aspect of the mother goddess Mahadevi. She is associated with protection, strength, motherhood, destruction, and wars.
